This Digital Literacy Project has been an invaluable journey in bridging the gap between academic knowledge and practical digital skills. As a first-year B.Tech CSE student specializing in Cyber Security at VIT Bhopal, I recognized the importance of building a robust, professional online presence early in my academic career. Setting up my GitHub repository not only taught me the fundamentals of version control but also provided a structured, organized space to showcase my technical journey.

Through these tasks, I developed a comprehensive digital portfolio. Optimizing my LinkedIn and Kaggle profiles taught me how to effectively highlight my skills in Python and web development, and properly present projects like "India Trends Pulse" and my upcoming "Vibe Cheque" web application. Creating the Digital Literacy Awareness Quiz using Google Forms enhanced my understanding of data collection, user interfaces, and peer education.

Furthermore, drafting targeted professional emails and formulating a social media etiquette checklist reinforced the nuances of formal digital communication. The cybercrime case study on identity theft allowed me to apply my cybersecurity interests to real-world scenarios, emphasizing the critical need for privacy awareness and data protection. Finally, collaborating with an AI to design an educational game demonstrated how generative tools can be actively leveraged for creative problem-solving.

Overall, this experience has solidified my technical foundation and equipped me with the professional etiquette and cybersecurity awareness necessary to succeed as a developer and a responsible digital citizen.
