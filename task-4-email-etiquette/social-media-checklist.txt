Digital Literacy: Social Media Do's and Don'ts

5 Do's:
1. Do use strong, unique passwords and enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA).
2. Do think before you post; remember your digital footprint is permanent.
3. Do use professional profile pictures on platforms like LinkedIn.
4. Do fact-check information before sharing it with others.
5. Do engage in respectful and positive discussions online.

5 Don'ts:
1. Don't share your passwords or sensitive personal details publicly.
2. Don't post offensive, discriminatory, or disrespectful content.
3. Don't engage with or respond to internet trolls or cyberbullies.
4. Don't tag others in photos or posts without their explicit permission.
5. Don't use social media to vent frustrations about professors or employers.
